# BUG: Search Bar Becomes Unresponsive After Switching to Images Tab

## Environment  
- Browser: Google Chrome  
- OS: Windows 11  
- Page: Google Search â€“ Images Tab  

---

## Preconditions  
- User is on the Google search page  
- User has performed an initial search  
- User is viewing results under the Images tab  

---

## Steps to Reproduce  
1. Navigate to https://www.google.com  
2. Enter any search query (e.g. "cats")  
3. Press Enter  
4. Click on the **Images** tab  
5. Click inside the search bar  
6. Attempt to type a new query  

---

## Expected Result  
The new query should appear in the search bar and allow editing/searching.  

---

## Actual Result  
The cursor flickers inside the search field and no characters appear when typing. The field becomes unresponsive.  

---

## Severity  
High  

---

## Priority  
High  

---

## Frequency  
Occurs every time (100%).
